Recipe

★★★★★

Ingredients

  • 2 slices spam - sliced 3/8" thick
  • 3 ounces cooked seasoned white rice
  • 1tsp furikaki seasoning
  • 1 whole sheet nori

Directions

  1. In large skillet, cook spam until lightly browned and crisp.
  2. Place rice into musubi press or line inside of empty spam can with plastic wrap and place rice in can. Press rice down firmly.
  3. Sprinkle with seasoned furikake and toasted sesame seeds, if desired.
  4. Place spam on rice in press or in can. Press down firmly.
  5. Remove SPAM® and rice from musubi press or can.
  6. On work surface, cut nori to desired width.
  7. Lay nori shiny-side-down; top with pressed spam and rice. Wrap nori fully around. Serve immediately.
